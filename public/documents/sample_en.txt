Employee Handbook

Section 1: Introduction
Welcome to the company! This handbook contains important information about company policies, procedures, and employee benefits.

Section 2: Work Hours and Attendance
Standard work hours are between 09:00 and 17:00, Monday through Friday.
Employees must arrive on time and maintain consistent attendance.
Work from home is available 2 days per week with manager approval.
If you are late, please notify your manager as soon as possible.

Section 3: Benefits Package
All full-time employees receive the following benefits:
- Comprehensive health insurance (medical, dental, and vision coverage)
- 401(k) retirement plan with 3% company match
- 20 paid days off per year
- Professional development budget of $2,000 per year
- Gym membership subsidy ($50 per month)
- Health Savings Account (HSA) with company contribution

Section 4: Vacation and Time Off
Employees are entitled to:
- 20 paid days off per year
- 5 sick days per year
- 10 holidays when the company is closed
- Personal days that can be used for emergencies

Section 5: Workplace Safety
We are committed to maintaining a safe workplace:
- All employees must follow safety procedures
- Hazards must be reported immediately to management
- Emergency exits must be kept clear
- First aid kits are located on every floor

Section 6: Code of Conduct
- Treat all employees with respect and professionalism
- Maintain confidentiality of company information
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Report any unethical behavior to HR management

Section 7: Work From Home Policy
Work from home is available with manager approval:
- Maximum 2 days per week
- Must have a dedicated workspace
- Must notify your team of your availability
- You may be required to come to the office for meetings

Section 8: Professional Development
The company supports employee growth and development:
- Annual learning budget of $2,000
- Tuition reimbursement program
- Participation in conferences and training
- Professional mentoring and coaching

Section 9: Pay and Compensation
- Employees are paid bi-weekly
- Direct deposit is required
- Raises are reviewed annually
- Performance bonuses may be available

Section 10: Termination and Resignation
Employment with the company is at-will:
- Either party can terminate employment with appropriate notice
- The standard notice period is two weeks
- Final pay includes unused vacation days
- An exit interview will be conducted
